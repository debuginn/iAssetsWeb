Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated · 2026-03-03

Welcome to iAssets. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how iAssets handles data.

1. Data We Collect

iAssets follows a local-first data model. Asset, liability, account, and category data you enter is stored on your device by default.

  • By default, data is not uploaded to our servers.

  • By default, we do not build identity-linked remote profiles.

2. Sync and Backup

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is synced across devices via Apple iCloud, subject to Apple’s terms and privacy policy.

3. Third-Party Services

We may rely on required third-party infrastructure (such as Apple platform capabilities) for core functions. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Security

We recommend enabling device passcode, biometric unlock, and system security settings. iAssets supports passcode protection and auto-lock.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Except where required by law or necessary for safety, we do not proactively disclose your local data content to third parties.

6. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated versions take effect when published. Material changes will be announced in app or on site.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: idebuginn@gmail.com
